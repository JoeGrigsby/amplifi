# AmpliFi — Brand Guidelines

> *for the song.*

---

## Brand Purpose

To give independent singer-songwriters the community, infrastructure, and resources they need to sustain a creative life — because great songs deserve more than the social media grind.

---

## Positioning

AmpliFi is an ecosystem engine — not a label, not a platform, not a management company. It's the connective tissue between artists, venues, studios, fans, and knowledge. A nonprofit built to solve structural problems, not celebrate them.

AmpliFi operates across four interconnected pillars:

| Pillar | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **Artist Collectives** | Community, accountability, creative momentum |
| **Songwriter Trail** | Curated touring circuit connecting artists to venues and fans |
| **Studio Network** | Subsidized recording access through partner studios |
| **Knowledge Hub** | Shared learning — one artist's breakthrough benefits all |

---

## Brand Architecture

AmpliFi is the **mission and the infrastructure**. Its programs — the Gruene Songwriters Collective, the Songwriter Trail, the Studio Network, the Knowledge Hub — are the expressions of that mission. The brand hierarchy flows as:

```
AmpliFi (nonprofit umbrella)
├── Artist Collectives (e.g. The Gruene Songwriters Collective)
├── The Songwriter Trail
├── The AmpliFi Studio Network
└── The AmpliFi Knowledge Hub
```

Each program has its own voice and visual identity, but all report to AmpliFi's core purpose: **for the song.**

## Tone of Voice

### Four Principles

**1. Purposeful**
Every word earns its place. No fluff, no hype. The work speaks. AmpliFi copy is written like a well-edited op-ed from someone who has actually played the venues and knows the problem from the inside.

**2. Advocate**
AmpliFi speaks on behalf of artists — not at them, not about them. Righteous without being self-righteous. The problem (isolated, invisible, underfunded) is named clearly. The solution is presented with specificity, not abstraction.

**3. Structural**
Names problems clearly. Presents solutions with specificity. AmpliFi is a systems-level organization and its language reflects that — without drifting into jargon or corporate-speak.

**4. Warm, not cozy**
Human and grounded. Never corporate, never precious. The warmth comes from genuine belief in the artists, not from brand personality performance.

- "Empowering creators"
- "A full-stack solution for artists"
- "Building community at scale"

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Midnight | `#1a1208` | Primary background, deep contrast |
| Gold | `#c8a45a` | Primary accent, headings, highlights |
| Parchment | `#f2ead8` | Light backgrounds, body text on dark |
| Ember | `#e05c2e` | CTA accent, active states |

### Typography
- **Display / Headlines:** Serif (Playfair Display or equivalent) — weight and elegance
- **Labels / Navigation:** Oswald — condensed, uppercase, tracked
- **Body:** Crimson Pro or similar humanist serif — warm, readable, literary

### Photography
- Black and white preferred
- Candid over posed
- Intimacy over spectacle
- Artists in natural environments: studios, stages, practice sessions, conversation

### Logo
The guitar amp as icon — utility meets craft. The logo signals that AmpliFi is a tool as much as a movement.

## What AmpliFi Is Not

- Not a streaming platform
- Not an artist management company
- Not a label or publisher
- Not a booking agency
- Not a music school

AmpliFi is the connective infrastructure between all of those things — the nonprofit backbone that makes the ecosystem function.
